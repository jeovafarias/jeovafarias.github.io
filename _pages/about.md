I am a fourth year PhD student in Computer Engineering at [Brown
University](https://www.brown.edu/) and I have the honor to be advised by Prof.
[Pedro Felzenszwalb](http://cs.brown.edu/people/pfelzens/). I earned my Masters
degree in Computer Science at [University of Nice-
Sophia Antipolis](http://unice.fr/) under Prof. [Marc
Antonini](http://www.i3s.unice.fr/~am/Biography.html). Prior to that, I
completed my Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering at [Federal University of
Ceara (UFC)](http://www.ufc.br/) under Prof. [Fátima N. S. de Medeiros](http://lattes.cnpq.br/5144730275745518).

I am broadly interested in unsupervised problems in Computer Vision and Data Science. In my
current research, I am interested in using discrete optimization and statistics
to tackle Image Segmentation. Previously, I studied problems involving Subspace
Clustering, Semidefinite Programing and Belief propagation. In my undergrad
years, my research focus was on SAR Image Segmentation and Level Sets.
