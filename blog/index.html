Blog

Jeova Farias Sales Rocha Neto

I am currently a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Haverford College. I received my PhD Computer Engineering at Brown University in 2021, where I had the honor to be advised by Prof. Pedro Felzenszwalb. I earned my Masters degree in Computer Science at University of Nice-Sophia Antipolis under Prof. Marc Antonini. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering at Federal University of Ceara (UFC) Prof. Fátima N. S. de Medeiros. I am broadly interested in unsupervised problems in Computer Vision and Data Science. In my current research, I am interested in using discrete optimization and statistics to tackle Image Segmentation. Previously, I studied problems involving Subspace Clustering, Semidefinite Programing and Belief propagation. In my undergrad years, my research focus was on SAR Image Segmentation and Level Sets.
